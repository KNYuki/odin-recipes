Bomb Quesadilla

Description

Homemade Quesadilla recipe using budget ingredients. Only takes 10minutes!

Ingredients

  • Spices
  • Ground Meat
  • Onions
  • Tomato Paste
  • Wraps
  • Cheese

Recipe Steps

  1. Prepare your spice mix
  2. Saute onions
  3. Add ground meat once onions are translucent
  4. Add in spices
  5. Once meat is browned, add in tomato paste
  6. Place filling into a bowl and clean the pan
  7. In a clean pan, spray some oil, place wrap down and cover with cheese
  8. Once cheese starts to melt, add in meat, then fold.
  9. Voila!
